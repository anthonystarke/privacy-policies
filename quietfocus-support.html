Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a focus session?

Pick a duration and category on the home screen, optionally choose an ambient sound, and tap Start. The timer will run in the background and notify you when the session is complete.

Can I pause and resume a session?

Yes. Tap the pause button to pause the timer at any time, then tap resume to continue. Reset will discard the current session.

Where is my session history?

Tap the history icon to see your completed sessions and streaks. The free version shows the last 7 days; QuietFocus Pro unlocks the full history.

Why didn't I get a completion notification?

Open the iOS Settings app → Notifications → QuietFocus and make sure notifications are allowed. QuietFocus uses a single local notification to let you know when your session ends.

What does QuietFocus Pro include?

Pro is a one-time purchase ($4.99, no subscription) that unlocks the full session history, the premium ambient sound library, and the premium completion sounds. All future Pro features are included.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Open the paywall (tap any Pro feature) and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. Your purchase is linked to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically on any device signed into the same account.

I bought Pro but the app still shows the free version.

Tap "Restore Purchases" on the paywall — QuietFocus only grants Pro after a verified transaction or an explicit restore. If the issue persists, email starke@gmail.com with your Apple ID region and approximate purchase date.

Does QuietFocus collect my data?

No. All sessions, streaks, and preferences are stored locally on your device using SQLite. QuietFocus has no servers, no analytics, and no tracking. See the privacy policy for full details.
