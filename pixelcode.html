PixelCode — Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 7, 2026

Overview

PixelCode is a personal QR code scanner and generator app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect (very little) and how it is handled.

Data Storage

All your scan history, generated QR codes, and settings — is stored locally on your device using SQLite and encrypted key-value storage. We do not operate servers that store your scan data. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it (e.g., CSV export).

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No cloud storage credentials
  • No location data
  • No contacts or photos
  • No tracking or advertising identifiers

In-App Purchases

PixelCode offers optional paid subscriptions through Apple's App Store. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID and purchase receipt from Apple — no personal information is shared. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at their website.

Analytics

PixelCode does not include any analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, or third-party tracking services.

Children's Privacy

PixelCode does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. The app does not require an account or collect personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
